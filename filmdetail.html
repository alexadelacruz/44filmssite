This project from #44Films takes a look at how everyone's favorite mascot, Otto Orange, came to be at Syracuse University!

44 Films is a partnership between Syracuse Athletics and the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communication, telling the stories of Orange sports through student-directed video projects.
